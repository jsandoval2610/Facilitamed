Requirements

Who can apply?

Students who are enrolled in medical school in Guatemala, El Salvador or Nicaragua

Requirements

  • Complete this form
  • Be an active medical student at any stage of your studies
  • Certificate of enrollment from your current university
  • Current certified transcript with all the courses that have been taken to date.
  • Not have failed any course
  • Have a personal bank account in a local bank with the student’s name
  • Detailed budget of monthly expenses (transportation, food, office materials, etc.)

Application Process

Fill out the application form and send it via email to facilitamed.ev@gmail.com

  • Your application will be reviewed and presented in the general assembly which takes place twice a year (before the beginning of each semester)
  • The final decision regarding your application will be sent via email
  • If your application is accepted, we will send a contract that needs to be signed and returned via email

Wire Transfers and Expenses

  • It is necessary to have a personal bank account, we will be making a wire transfer at the beginning of each semester to this account
  • You will be required to maintain an accurate record of your expenses which will need to be sent to us via email

What kind of help will you get?

  • If your application is accepted, you will be awarded a scholarship of 100€ (euro) per month or its equivalent in local currency
  • The scholarship will have a maximum duration of 6 years or until you are finished with your studies (whichever comes first)
  • Failing any academic courses immediately terminates your scholarship.
